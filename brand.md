# ChronoLab brand

Status: active

## Direction

Stark-minimal developer infrastructure with an industrial terminal core. Spacious marketing rhythm surrounds compact, precise command surfaces.

## Palette

- Canvas: warm near-black (`oklch(0.17 0.01 80)`)
- Surface: warm charcoal (`oklch(0.205 0.012 78)`)
- Ink: soft warm white (`oklch(0.94 0.012 85)`)
- Muted ink: warm gray (`oklch(0.72 0.015 80)`)
- Accent: clock amber (`oklch(0.78 0.15 77)`)
- Success: restrained green (`oklch(0.76 0.12 150)`)

Amber represents logical time and primary action. Green is reserved for verified success. No competing decorative hues.

## Typography

- Interface and display: IBM Plex Sans Variable
- Commands, timestamps, and technical labels: IBM Plex Mono
- Display headings use tight tracking and restrained weight.

## Voice

Direct, technical, calm, and specific. Prefer verifiable statements such as “Host clock untouched” over aspirational marketing language.

## Motion

Crisp and functional. Use 100–150ms property-specific transitions with a custom ease-out curve. Disable non-essential motion when reduced motion is requested.
